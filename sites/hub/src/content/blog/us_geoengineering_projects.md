## 🇺🇸 U.S.-Based Geoengineering Projects (2025)

Here’s a breakdown of major **geoengineering projects based in the United States**, from public academic research to speculative black budget programs.

### ✅ Known Public Projects

#### **NOAA – Earth Radiation Budget Initiative**

- **Type:** Government research (non-deployment)
- **Focus:** Solar radiation, cloud/aerosol interaction
- **Oversight:** Federal
- **Status:** Active research, no field trials

#### **Harvard Solar Geoengineering Research Program (SGRP)**

- **Key Proposal:** Stratospheric Controlled Perturbation Experiment (SCoPEx)
- **Status:** Canceled due to public backlash
- **Focus:** Aerosol modeling and ethics
- **Funding:** Private philanthropy

#### **Project Vesta**

- **Type:** Coastal carbon removal via olivine weathering
- **Sites:** Planned in New York and North Carolina
- **Goal:** Sequester CO₂ through enhanced ocean alkalinity

#### **Arctic Ice Project**

- **Type:** Arctic albedo restoration
- **Method:** Reflective silica microspheres on sea ice
- **Status:** Small-scale trials, lab simulations

#### **U.S. DOE & National Labs**

- **Role:** Modeling, climate simulation, aerosol physics
- **Agencies:** Lawrence Livermore, Oak Ridge, etc.
- **No active deployment**

### 🕵️ Could There Be Classified Projects?

There’s **no public proof**, but based on history and strategic interests:

- **Precedent:** U.S. ran weather modification in Vietnam (*Operation Popeye*) and other atmospheric programs (*HAARP*, *Project Stormfury*).
- **Motivation:** National security, disaster mitigation, weather dominance.
- **Concealment:** Classified funding through **DARPA, CIA, or DOD** could obscure such programs.

🚨 **Bottom Line:** While no classified geoengineering projects are confirmed, it's highly plausible that **undisclosed modeling or dual-use tests exist**.
